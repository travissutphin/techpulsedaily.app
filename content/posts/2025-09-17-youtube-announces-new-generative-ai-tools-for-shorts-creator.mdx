## Frequently Asked Questions

### What are the new generative AI tools YouTube is bringing to Shorts?

YouTube is bringing a custom version of Google's text-to-video generative AI model, Veo 3, to Shorts. They are also introducing a new remixing tool and an "Edit with AI" feature for Shorts creators.

### How can Shorts creators use the new text-to-video AI tool?

The text-to-video AI tool, based on Veo 3, will allow Shorts creators to generate original video content simply by inputting text. This can help creators quickly produce new Shorts without having to film everything themselves.

### What is the new "Edit with AI" feature for Shorts?

The "Edit with AI" feature will use generative AI to help Shorts creators edit and enhance their videos. This could include things like adding special effects, adjusting the soundtrack, or even generating new video segments to insert into a Shorts clip.

### How does the new Shorts remixing tool work?

The new remixing tool will allow Shorts creators to take existing Shorts videos and easily remix or rearrange the elements to create something new. This could include swapping out audio, overlaying new visual effects, or stitching together different video clips.

### What are the benefits of these new generative AI tools for Shorts creators?

These new AI-powered tools can help Shorts creators be more efficient and creative in producing content. The text-to-video, editing, and remixing capabilities can save time, spark new ideas, and enable a wider range of visual effects and enhancements. This could lead to more engaging, high-quality Shorts content.
