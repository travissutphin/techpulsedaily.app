## Summary

Key Takeaway:
The rising popularity of AI is driving a significant increase in electricity demand, with the potential to reshape the power grid. However, some argue that AI could also help get more clean power online and run the grid more efficiently.

Why it Matters:
The energy consumption of data centers powering AI has gone up by 80% from 2020 to 2025 and is likely to keep growing, potentially leading to higher electricity prices. Understanding the impact of AI on the grid and exploring ways it could be a positive force are crucial.

Brief Context:
MIT Technology Review has investigated the climate and energy burden of AI, including how much energy leading AI models use. While companies have been reluctant to share this information, some are now starting to release the data. The article also discusses new ways to interpret how AI systems work, and provides a roundup of the latest tech news.
